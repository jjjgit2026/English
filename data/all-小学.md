# 小学生英语范文

## 1. 自我介绍

**Title: Self-introduction**

**Body:**
Hello, everyone! My name is Li Ming. I am 10 years old. I am a student at Sunshine Primary School. I have a happy family: my father, my mother and me. I like reading books and playing basketball. I hope we can be good friends. Thank you!

**中文大意:**
大家好！我叫李明。我今年10岁了。我是阳光小学的一名学生。我有一个幸福的家庭：我的爸爸，我的妈妈和我。我喜欢读书和打篮球。我希望我们能成为好朋友。谢谢！

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
hello, everyone, name, old, student, school, have, happy, family, father, mother, like, reading, book, playing, basketball, hope, friend, thank

**语法点:**

- 一般现在时的自我介绍
- 表达喜好的句型：`I like...`
- 简单并列句

## 2. 我的家庭

**Title: My Family**

**Body:**
There are three people in my family: my father, my mother, and me. My father is a teacher. He is tall and kind. My mother is a doctor. She is busy but always takes care of me. I love my family very much.

**中文大意:**
我们是一个三口之家：我的爸爸，我的妈妈和我。我的爸爸是一名老师。他又高又和善。我的妈妈是一名医生。她很忙但总是照顾我。我非常爱我的家庭。

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**father, mother, teacher, tall, kind, doctor, busy, take care of, love, very much

**语法点:**

- 描述人物外貌和性格的形容词
- 用`and`和`but`连接并列成分

## 3. 我的朋友

**Title: My Friend**

**Body:**
My best friend is Wang Fang. She is 10 years old, just like me. She has big eyes and long hair. She is good at drawing. We often go to school together and share our snacks. I am happy to have such a good friend.

**中文大意:**
我最好的朋友是王芳。她和我一样，也是10岁。她有大眼睛和长头发。她擅长画画。我们经常一起去上学，并且分享我们的零食。我很高兴有这样一个好朋友。

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
best friend, old, like, have, big, eyes, long, hair, good at, drawing, often, go to school, together, share, snack, happy, such

**语法点:**

- 描述人物外貌的句型：`She has...`
- 表达能力的句型：`be good at...`
- 频率副词`often`的使用

## 4. 我的学校

**Title: My School**

**Body:**
My school is Green Tree Primary School. It is big and beautiful. There is a playground, a library and many classrooms. My favorite place is the library because I can read many interesting books there. I love my school.

**中文大意:**
我的学校是绿树小学。它又大又漂亮。学校里有一个操场，一个图书馆和许多教室。我最喜欢的地方是图书馆，因为我可以在那里读很多有趣的书。我爱我的学校。

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
school, big, beautiful, there is, playground, library, classroom, favorite, place, because, can, read, interesting, book, love

**语法点:**

- 用`There be`句型描述场所
- 用`because`引导原因状语从句
- 表达喜好的形容词：`favorite`

## 5. 我最喜欢的季节

**Title: My Favorite Season**

**Body:**
My favorite season is spring. In spring, the weather is warm. Flowers bloom and birds sing. I can fly kites with my friends in the park. Sometimes we have a picnic. Spring makes me happy and energetic.

**中文大意:**
我最喜欢的季节是春天。在春天，天气很温暖。花儿开放，鸟儿歌唱。我可以和我的朋友们在公园里放风筝。有时我们还会去野餐。春天让我感到快乐和充满活力。

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
favorite, season, spring, weather, warm, flower, bloom, bird, sing, can, fly a kite, friend, park, sometimes, have a picnic, make, happy, energetic

**语法点:**

- 描述季节和天气
- 用`can`表达能够做的事情
- 使役动词`make`的用法：`make me happy`

## 6. 我的假期

**Title: My Holiday**

**Body:**
Last summer holiday, I went to Beijing with my parents. We visited the Great Wall. It is very long and old. We also ate Beijing roast duck. It was delicious! I took many photos. I had a wonderful time.

**中文大意:**
上个暑假，我和我的父母去了北京。我们参观了长城。它很长很古老。我们还吃了北京烤鸭。它非常美味！我拍了许多照片。我度过了一段美妙的时光。

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
last, summer, holiday, went, Beijing, parent, visited, Great Wall, long, old, also, ate, roast duck, delicious, took, photo, had, wonderful, time

**语法点:**

- 一般过去时描述过去的事件
- 常见不规则动词的过去式：`go-went`, `eat-ate`, `take-took`, `have-had`

## 7. 我的爱好

**Title: My Hobby**

**Body:**
I have many hobbies, but my favorite is reading. I read storybooks every day. Reading makes me smart and happy. I also like swimming. I go swimming with my father on Sundays. Hobbies make my life colorful.

**中文大意:**
我有很多爱好，但我最喜欢的是阅读。我每天都读故事书。阅读让我变得聪明和快乐。我也喜欢游泳。我每个星期天和爸爸一起去游泳。爱好让我的生活丰富多彩。

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
have, hobby, but, favorite, reading, read, storybook, every day, make, smart, happy, also, like, swimming, go swimming, father, Sunday, life, colorful

**语法点:**

- 用`but`表示转折
- 动名词作主语：`Reading makes me...`
- 频率表达：`every day`, `on Sundays`

## 8. 我的宠物

**Title: My Pet**

**Body:**
I have a lovely pet. It is a white cat. Its name is Snowball. It has blue eyes and a long tail. Snowball likes to play with a ball. It is my best friend. I take care of it every day.

**中文大意:**
我有一只可爱的宠物。它是一只白色的猫。它的名字叫雪球。它有蓝色的眼睛和一条长尾巴。雪球喜欢玩球。它是我最好的朋友。我每天都照顾它。

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
have, lovely, pet, white, cat, its, name, blue, eyes, long, tail, likes, play with, ball, best friend, take care of, every day

**语法点:**

- 用`it`和`its`指代宠物
- 描述动物特征的句型
- 动词短语：`like to do`, `play with`, `take care of`

## 9. 我的一天

**Title: My Day**

**Body:**
I get up at 7 o'clock every day. After breakfast, I go to school. I have four classes in the morning. After school, I do my homework. In the evening, I watch TV with my family. I go to bed at 9 p.m. I have a busy but happy day.

**中文大意:**
我每天7点起床。早饭后，我去上学。我上午有四节课。放学后，我做作业。晚上，我和家人一起看电视。我晚上9点上床睡觉。我度过了忙碌但快乐的一天。

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
get up, o‘clock, every day, after, breakfast, go to school, have, class, morning, do, homework, evening, watch TV, family, go to bed, busy, happy, day

**语法点:**

- 用一般现在时描述日常作息
- 时间顺序连接词：`after`, `in the evening`
- 用`but`连接两个形容词

## 10. 我的梦想

**Title: My Dream**

**Body:**
When I grow up, I want to be a scientist. I want to invent useful things to help people. I will study hard to make my dream come true. I believe nothing is impossible if I work hard.

**中文大意:**
当我长大后，我想成为一名科学家。我想要发明有用的东西来帮助人们。我会努力学习，让我的梦想成真。我相信，只要我努力，一切皆有可能。

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
when, grow up, want, be, scientist, invent, useful, thing, help, people, will, study hard, make, dream come true, believe, nothing, impossible, if, work hard

**语法点:**

- 用`when`引导时间状语从句
- 表达愿望的句型：`I want to be...`
- 用`will`表达未来意愿
- 条件状语从句：`if I work hard`
